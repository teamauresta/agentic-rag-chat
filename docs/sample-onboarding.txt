New Employee Onboarding Guide — Acme Corp

Welcome to Acme Corp! This guide covers everything you need to get started in your first week.

Day 1: Setup
- Collect your laptop from IT (Level 3, Room 302)
- Set up your email and Slack accounts (credentials in your welcome email)
- Complete mandatory security awareness training (30 minutes, online)
- Meet your buddy — they'll show you around the office

Day 2-3: Tools & Access
- Request access to GitHub, Jira, and Confluence via the IT portal (it.acmecorp.internal)
- Set up your development environment using the dev-setup script: ./scripts/setup-dev.sh
- Review the Engineering Handbook in Confluence
- Complete your first PR — even if it's just fixing a typo. Ship something!

Day 4-5: Team Integration
- Attend your team's standup and sprint planning
- Schedule 1:1 coffee chats with 3-4 team members
- Review the current sprint board and pick up a starter ticket (tagged "good-first-issue")
- Set up your 30/60/90 day goals with your manager

Key Policies
- Flexible hours: Core hours are 10am-3pm AEST. Work the rest when it suits you.
- Remote work: Up to 3 days per week after your first month.
- Leave: 20 days annual leave + 10 days personal/sick leave.
- Learning budget: $2,000/year for courses, conferences, and books.

Key Contacts
- IT Support: it@acmecorp.example.com or Slack #it-help
- HR: hr@acmecorp.example.com
- Facilities: facilities@acmecorp.example.com
- Your manager: Check your welcome email for details
